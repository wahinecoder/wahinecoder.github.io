Trip'in Chicago

Let Our Concierge Plan Your Daily Schedule and Route

Trip'in Chicago is a perfect travel app for visitors and residents alike. The app enables users to browse through sights, restaurants, and entertainment near them. Furthermore, users can customize their day by using the Conceirge, an app function that allows users to customize venues and routes based on location.

  • Date: May 2014
  • Development Team: Marion Ano, Jaime Hernandez, and Jay Ang
  • Category: iOS Development

KiloBooks

Helping Children to Observe, Experience, and Forecast

Kilo Books cultivates the naturalist in young children by strengthening their inherent observational and forecasting skills through the native Hawaiian practice of Kilo. Our books root children in the native Hawaiian practice of kilo, which means to watch closely, spy, examine, look around, observe and forecast.

  • Date: June 2019
  • Client: Kilo Books LLC
  • Category: Branding
  • Designer: Aline Design Hawaii

Loko Iʻa

Technology-Enabled and Place-Based Learning with an Emphasis on Integrating Science and Traditional Knowledge

Loko iʻa allows users to explore and learn about a Native Hawaiian Fishpond through guided on-site and virtual tours including audio interviews with Fishpond Experts. New functionality includes Real-Time Data from water quality sensors in the Fishpond. New app sections include information on Mea Ola (living organisms), and ʻIke Hawaiʻi (traditional Hawaiian knowledge)

  • Date: August 2017
  • Development Team: Marion Ano, Jaime Hernandez, and Dennis Walsh
  • Client: University of Hawaiʻi-Judy Lemus
  • Category: iOS Development
  • Designer: Aline Design Hawaii
