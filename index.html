Loko Iʻa

This is the second version of the Loko iʻa app published in August 2017. Loko iʻa allows you to explore and learn about a Native Hawaiian fishpond through guided on-site and virtual tours and audio interviews with Fishpond experts. New features in 2.0 include Real-Time Data from water quality sensors in the fishpond,
Mea Ola-pond life, and ʻIke Hawaiʻi-Hawaiian Knowledge

  • Client: University of Hawaiʻi-Judy Lemus
  • Date: August 2017
  • Service: iOS Development

Trip'in Chicago

Trip'in Chicago is the perfect travel app for visitors and residents alike allowing users to browse through sights, food, and entertainment. Furthermore, users can plan their day using the Concierge, which allows users to customize sightseeing right from the user's location.

  • Development Team: Marion Ano, Jaime Hernandez, and Jay Ang
  • Date: May 2014
  • Service: iOS Development
